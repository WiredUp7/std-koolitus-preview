Privacy policy

Last updated: 21.06.2026

Look360 is a monitoring and automation service whose core is watching and organising your data. That is why we take data protection seriously. This policy explains what personal data and customer data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Draft for review. Before public use, STD Koolitus OÜ will have it checked by a lawyer and fill in the actual registry details (registry code, address).

1.Data controller

The Look360 service is provided by STD Koolitus OÜ (registry code 16234567), address Riia 15b, 51010 Tartu, Estonia. For data protection questions, write to info@look360.eu.

2.What data we process

2.1 Client contact details

On sign-up and while providing the service: company name, contact person's name, email address, phone number. Legal basis: conclusion and performance of the contract (GDPR Art 6(1)(b)).

2.2 Access needed to provide the service

To carry out monitoring, with your permission we access the sources you choose to monitor. We use read-only access and do not change your data:

  • your e-shop (e.g. WooCommerce) products, prices, stock and content;
  • your ERP system (e.g. Directo) products and stock, if you order the ERP integration;
  • your Google Search Console search positions, if you order SEO monitoring;
  • public data from the websites and Facebook pages of the competitors you specify.

About competitors we collect only publicly available information (public product prices, stock, public posts). We do not collect personal data about competitors.

2.3 Technical data

When using the monitoring panel: login time, activity logs and technical logs to ensure the security and reliability of the service (legitimate interest, GDPR Art 6(1)(f)).

PurposeLegal basis

4.Retention

We keep client contact details for the duration of the contract and for up to 3 years after it ends. Accounting source documents we keep for 7 years as required by law. Monitoring data (history, trends) we hold to provide the service for the duration of the contract; when the contract ends we delete it within 90 days, unless you ask sooner.

5.Who we share with

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share your data with your competitors or other clients. Only those Look360 staff who need it for their work can see the data.

To provide the service we use trusted sub-processors (e.g. cloud hosting, the Google API for sending emails). We have a data processing agreement with every sub-processor. All data is processed in the European Union or with an equivalent level of protection.

6.Data processing agreement (DPA)

If we process your customer or business data on your behalf, we conclude a separate data processing agreement (DPA) with you, setting out the scope, duration and security measures of the processing in accordance with GDPR Art 28. To request a DPA, write to info@look360.eu.

7.Your rights

You have the right to:

  • ask what data we process about you and receive a copy of it;
  • request the correction of inaccurate data;
  • request the erasure of data ("the right to be forgotten");
  • restrict processing or object to it;
  • receive your data in a portable format;
  • withdraw consent and cancel the service at any time.

You can always revoke access yourself. Because we use read-only access, you can revoke it at any time in your own system (WooCommerce, Directo, Google).

To exercise your rights, write to info@look360.eu.

If you believe we have infringed your rights, you can contact the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate: aki.ee.

8.Cookies

The Look360 website uses only technically necessary cookies (e.g. the login session). We use analytics or marketing cookies only with your consent. You can change your consent at any time.

9.Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures: encrypted connections (HTTPS), read-only access (we cannot change your systems), access restriction and logging. No system is 100% secure, but we make reasonable efforts to protect your data.

10.Changes and contact

We may update this policy from time to time. We notify clients of significant changes by email. Questions: info@look360.eu.
